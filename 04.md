# Chapter 4

The Way is empty, yet inexhaustible.  
Deep source of all things.

It blunts our edges,  
untangles our knots,  
softens our glare,  
joins us with the dust of the world.

Hidden, but ever present.  
I do not know where it comes from.  
It is the ancestor of all.
